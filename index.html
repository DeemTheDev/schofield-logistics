Who are we

Established in 2018, Schofield Logistics are precursors in transportation brokerage, having solid credentials and a dedicated employee compliment that have taken pride in providing quality service standards. From humble beginnings, the enterprise started with a miniscule client base and has since recorded substantial growth. Primarily the company’s successes are due to providing every transport need under one organization in an ever increasing and demanding transport logistics market. With a growing list of associations and a formidable client base, Schofield Logistics is truly a unique company at the forefront of the industry; and one that is evolutionary in its development, thinking and foresight.

Our Mission

It is the mission of Schofield Logistics to innovate, develop, implement and manage logistics, transport and distribution solutions through learning and understanding our customers business. To achieve this, it is our goal to constantly strive to be increasingly innovative, flexible and open with all business partners, customers and suppliers

BUSINESS VALUES

Values form the foundation of a business, hence our values are designed to guide business activity and reflect the energy and spirit of a company that has a solid foundation to lead change as business evolves. These values include:

  • The ability to conduct business in an ethical and moral manner
  • The continuous improvement of the organizations performance
  • Ensuring compliance in regularity and legal issues
  • The commitment to successfully provide our customers with services and expertise that remain unequalled, while simultaneously exceeding industry standards
  • To continuously seek knowledge and acquire expertise to transfer advice onto customers, subcontractors and colleagues

Social Responsibility

Schofield Logistics is grounded in their commitment to take into account the social needs and issues impacting communities around Kwazulu Natal. As the companies philanthropic arm, we have invested in contributing to feeding schemes as well as charities within our vicinity. This commitment to our community and country as a whole motivates us as a company. Thus, we have been and continue to be an agent of change, balancing societies needs with our mission to build a successful and profitable business.
